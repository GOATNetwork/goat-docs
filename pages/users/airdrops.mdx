# GOAT Network Airdrops Claim Guide

GOAT Network distributes rewards across several tracks. Each track has its own eligibility criteria, reward asset, and claim window. This guide walks through how to claim from each one.

---

## I. Airdrop Types and Eligibility

### Community Airdrop - One Piece Season 4 — Phase 2

- **Reward token:** USDT (bridged stgUSDT on GOAT Network).
- **Eligibility:** Addresses that participated in the Bridge, GoatUp, and Oku interaction tasks during **One Piece Season 4 — Phase 2 (Dec 19, 2025 – Apr 17, 2026)**.
- **Claim window:** **April 22, 2026 12:00 UTC → July 21, 2026 12:00 UTC**.
- Unclaimed USDT returns to the GOAT Treasury after the deadline.

---

## II. Preparations Before Claiming

### 1. Wallet Setup

- Prepare an **EVM-compatible wallet address** (OKX, MetaMask, Bitget, Coinbase Wallet, OneKey, WalletConnect, etc.).
- For best experience, use a **browser extension wallet on desktop**.
- Mobile wallets (via WalletConnect) are supported, but may be less stable.

### 2. Gas Fee Preparation

- Transactions on GOAT Network require **Native BTC Gas**.
- Minimum gas per claim: about **$0.01**.
- Recommended: at least **0.0000001 BTC Gas**.
- Top up via the official bridge: [GOAT Bridge](https://bridge.goat.network/bridge).
- Bridge User Guide: [Bridge Guide](/users/bridge).

### 3. Network Setup

If the network is not auto-added, configure it manually:

- **RPC URL:** [https://rpc.ankr.com/goat_mainnet](https://rpc.ankr.com/goat_mainnet)
- **Chain ID:** 2345
- **Network Name:** GOAT Network
- **Currency Symbol:** BTC

## III. Community Airdrop Claim Process

### 1. Open the claim page

1. Open [airdrops.goat.network](https://airdrops.goat.network) and select the **Community Contributors Rewards** tab.
2. Click **Connect Wallet** and connect your EVM wallet (MetaMask, OKX Wallet, WalletConnect, etc.).
3. The page automatically runs an eligibility check and prompts you to sign a wallet-verification message. This verification step does **not** consume gas.

<p align="center">
  <img src="/img/user-guides/airdrop/airdrops_image_1.png" alt="Community Contributors Rewards dashboard" />
</p>

### 2. Review your allocation and submit the claim

1. If eligible, your USDT allocation appears on the claim panel.
2. Click **Claim**.
3. Read the Terms dialog and confirm the transaction in your wallet.
4. Pay a small BTC gas fee and wait for on-chain confirmation.

<p align="center">
  <img src="/img/user-guides/airdrop/airdrops_image_2.png" alt="USDT allocation and claim button" />
</p>

### 3. Claim success and post-claim actions

1. After confirmation, a success screen shows the claimed amount.
2. Click **Share on X** if you want to share the result.
3. Click **Add $USDT to Wallet** to register USDT in your wallet for easier tracking.

If you already claimed, the page detects the on-chain state and shows **You have successfully claimed all your allocated tokens!** together with the historical amount.

## IV. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q1: Who's eligible for the airdrop?**  
**One Piece Season 4 — Phase 2 (Dec 19, 2025 – Apr 17, 2026).** Eligible addresses are the ones that participated in the Bridge, GoatUp, and Oku interaction tasks during this campaign window.

**Q2: When can I claim my rewards?**  
The claim period is from **April 22, 2026 12:00 UTC to July 21, 2026 12:00 UTC**. The claim window remains open for 90 days.

**Q3: Which network should I use to claim tokens?**  
Use **GOAT Network** (chain ID **2345**). Switch your wallet to GOAT Network before signing the claim transaction. Supported wallets usually prompt the switch automatically.

**Q4: Do I need to pay gas fees to claim tokens?**  
Yes. Claiming is an on-chain transaction, so you pay the normal GOAT Network gas fee in **BTC**. There are no extra platform fees beyond gas.

**Q5: What happens if I miss the claiming period?**  
Unclaimed tokens are permanently returned to the GOAT Treasury after the deadline and can no longer be retrieved.

**Q6: How do I add USDT to my wallet after claiming?**  
After the success modal appears, click **Add $USDT to Wallet** and approve the wallet prompt if your wallet supports asset registration.
