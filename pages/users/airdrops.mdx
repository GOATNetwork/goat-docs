# GOAT Network Airdrops Claim Guide

This guide is intended for all users preparing to claim the **GOATED token airdrop** on GOAT Network. It covers **Community Airdrop**, **Top Yapper**, **StakeStone Rewards**, and Developer Airdrop (coming soon), including eligibility criteria, timelines, and claim procedures. Please read carefully to ensure a smooth process.

---

## I. Airdrop Types and Eligibility

### 1. Community Airdrop - Christmas gift

- Target: Users who participated in the **One Piece on-chain and social tasks**.
- Available for claim starting **Dec 27, 10:00 UTC**.
- Users have 90 days from the Dec 27 start date to claim their airdrop tokens. Tokens that remain unclaimed after 90 days will be permanently returned to the GOAT Treasury and will not be reissued.

### 2. Top Yapper Monthly Reward

- Yappers earn monthly leaderboard rewards, and eligible Yappers can come to claim their airdrop rewards.

### 3. StakeStone Rewards

- Target: Users who participated in GOAT Network Pre-Deposit on StakeStone.
- Rewards will be issued in batches. Please pay attention to the snapshot times.

### 4. Developer Airdrop (coming soon)

- Target: GOAT Network open source contributors on **GitHub**.
- Eligibility requires binding a GitHub account for verification.
- Available for claim: Date TBA.

**Note:** A single wallet may qualify for multiple airdrops. Entry points, verification methods, and claim procedures differ across airdrop types.

## II. Preparations Before Claiming

### 1. Wallet Setup

- Prepare an **EVM-compatible wallet address** (OKX, MetaMask, Bitget, Coinbase Wallet, OneKey, WalletConnect, etc.).
- For best experience, use a **browser extension wallet on desktop**.
- Mobile wallets (via WalletConnect) are supported, but may be less stable.

### 2. Gas Fee Preparation

- Transactions on GOAT Network require **Native BTC Gas**.
- Minimum gas per claim: about **$0.01**.
- Recommended: at least **0.0000001 BTC Gas**.
- Top-up via the official bridge: [GOAT Bridge](https://bridge.goat.network/bridge).
- Bridge User Guide: [Bridge Guide](/users/bridge).

### 3. Network Setup

If the network is not auto-added, configure manually:

- **RPC URL:** [https://rpc.ankr.com/goat_mainnet](https://rpc.ankr.com/goat_mainnet)  
- **Chain ID:** 2345  
- **Network Name:** GOAT Network  
- **Currency Symbol:** BTC
